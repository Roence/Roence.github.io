Partnering with governments, nonprofits, United Nations agencies, conservation organizations, and private sector companies, Roence Youth Foundation equips youths in Northern Uganda with resources and skills to savings groups and engaging in climate-smart economic activites. We aim to make positive living attractive. As one body with the community, we uphold our commitment to diversity, inclusion & equity, and consider them essential elements of a vibrant environment and fundamental to our organizationâ€™s mission. As an initiative who is tasked with changing the odds for high-potential teens from within the communities we are determined to foster an environment of inclusion, diversity, openness, and respect. We embrace and celebrate the differences including disability, immigration status, religion, gender, age, socioeconomic status, language, sexual orientation, and ethnicity. Roence Youth Foundation provides young people with opportunity, free from judgement and helps them envision a future worth investing in. And even more importantly, we encourage them to make a difference with the limited resources available and to pursue their dreams.
